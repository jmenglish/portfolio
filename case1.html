Personas


The following personas gave voice to these participants in the following ways: demographics, motivations, and frustrations. The results of the survey findings were gathered and reviewed to create personas this product will target. This information was used to design the features of this product to address participant's needs.


Persona 1

Full-Time Employee
Age: 35
Location: Boston, MA


The everyday user(s) is social, creative, and enjoys sharing information with others. He’s mostly interested in uploading and storing pictures and documents to share for personal use. His second priority is brainstorming and sharing content with team members on various projects.

Persona 2

Student
Age: 22
Location: Orlando, FL


The student is most interested in features that complement her needs making student life more manageable. She wants features for note taking, storing course documents, organizing study materials, and/or create and collaborate on projects.
